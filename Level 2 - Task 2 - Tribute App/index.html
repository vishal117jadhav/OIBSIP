Tribute To Virat Kohli

Biography

Virat Kohli, a renowned Indian cricketer, is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of the sport. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, Kohli's journey in cricket began at a young age, and he rapidly rose through the ranks with his extraordinary talent and dedication.

Early Life and Cricketing Journey

Virat Kohli developed a passion for cricket from a tender age and honed his skills at the West Delhi Cricket Academy. His prolific performances in domestic cricket caught the attention of selectors, leading to his U-19 selection for India. Kohli played a pivotal role in the victorious U-19 World Cup campaign in 2008, showcasing his potential on the world stage.

Career

  • Kohli's leadership skills came to the fore when he was appointed the captain of the Indian Test team in 2014 and later became the limited-overs captain as well. Under his captaincy, India achieved numerous historic series victories at home and abroad, attesting to his tactical acumen and ability to inspire his team to perform at their best.
